ABOUT THE APP

Workout & Log was created in September 2017 by Petter, Helen, and Daniel. It was their final project in the accelerated learning program in C#.NET at Academy, a part of Academic Work.

The goal was to create a simple app to not only easily track your workouts, but also encourage physical activity. With 3 months intensive programming, the Workout & Log was born during the program's last two weeks as the trios final project.

Our goal is to maintain and continue to develop the app. As the three of us goes our separate ways after the accelerated learning program, our wish is to keep on improving and adding features to Workout & Log.

Should you have any questions, you are welcome to contact us at woandlog@gmail.com.

If you want to support the project, you are very welcome to donate to us, as Workout & Log is something we do without pay, on our free time here on after. Thank you!
